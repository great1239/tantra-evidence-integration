# TMS Convergence Proof

## Flow

`Governance Decision -> Convergence Status`

## Convergence

```json
{"convergence_id":"tms_convergence_25ae37b577a517bd3d13","reason_codes":[],"status":"CONVERGED","trace_id":"trace_b996d7b5e3d19d0967f0"}
```

## Supported Statuses

`CONVERGED`, `PARTIAL`, `FAILED`

## Status Policy

`CONVERGED` means SHAKTI approved the evidence, MDU registration completed, and lineage reconstruction is available. `PARTIAL` means SHAKTI approved the evidence but MDU registration or reconstruction is incomplete. `FAILED` means SHAKTI did not approve validation.
